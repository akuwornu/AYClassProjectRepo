AUSTIN, Aug. 30 -- TexasOnline.com issued the following news release:

In the fast-paced world of technology procurement, time is of the essence. That's where DIR Cooperative Contracts
step in-helping you get what you need, right when you need it. But what exactly are DIR Cooperating Contracts,
and how can they benefit your organization?

Understanding Cooperative Contracts

DIR Cooperative Contracts are a streamlined solution for accessing information technology (IT) products and
services. Designed to provide easy access to products and solutions from DIR-approved vendors, these contracts
save you time and resources by simplifying the procurement process. With competitively bid contracts that are
compliant with purchasing laws, DIR ensures that you get the best value for your technology needs. Plus, with a
customer-driven approach, you're guaranteed tailored IT solutions that meet your organization's specific
requirements.

Benefits of Cooperative Contracts

Why should you consider DIR Cooperative Contracts? Here's why!

Cost savings: Leverage the state's buying power to access lower prices and best value options.

Efficiency: Streamlined purchasing processes reduces the overall purchase process time and eliminates duplicative
efforts, allowing you to focus on your core mission.

Diversity and inclusion: Access to Historically Underutilized Businesses (HUBs) promotes diversity and inclusion in
procurement practices.
