ALEXANDRIA, Va., Aug. 13 -- The trademark FLOODBASE (Serial No. 97751023) was published on Aug. 13,
2024, by USPTO in the Principal Register.

Owner(s): FLOODBASE, PBC; 1 DOCK 72 WAY BROOKLYN, NEW YORK 11205 Mark Information: Standard
Character Claim - Yes. The mark consists of standard characters without claim to any particular font style, size, or
color. Mark Drawing Type - 4 - STANDARD CHARACTER MARK Goods and Services: US Class(es):100, 101, 102

software used for providing flood data and tracking floods; providing temporary use of online, non-downloadable

designing, underwriting, monitoring, and triggering parametric flood insurance policies; provision of reports in
